Pizza

Description

Some delicious italian style, oven baked, classic Pizza.

ingredients

  • Pizza dough.
  • Red sauce.
  • Pepperoni.
  • Mozzarella cheese.
  • Italian seasoning.

Steps

  1. Lay out the dough.
  2. Put red sauce on the dough.
  3. Put cheese on the sauce.
  4. Put on pepperonis.
  5. Add italian seasoning.
  6. Cook dish in oven.
