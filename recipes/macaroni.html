Macaroni

Description

Some delicious italian style, oven baked, classic Macaroni.

ingredients

  • Noodles.
  • Cheese sauce.
  • Italian seasoning.

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Boil the cheese sauce.
  3. Drain the noodles.
  4. Combine ingredients into pan.
  5. Add italian seasoning.
  6. Cook dish in oven.
