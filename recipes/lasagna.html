Lasagna

Description

Some delicious italian style, oven baked, classic lasagna.

ingredients

  • Noodles.
  • Red sauce.
  • Ground beef.
  • Italian seasoning.

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Boil the red sauce.
  3. Pan fry the ground beef.
  4. Combine ingredients into pan.
  5. Add italian seasoning.
  6. Cook dish in oven.
